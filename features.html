Features

Elevate comes loaded with features to help your server. It can keep users active in chat with fun commands, and moderation commands can keep your server safe

Moderation

With tons of moderation commands, you don't have to worry about your server getting raided or repeatedly spammed. Elevate has your standard commands like kick & ban, and also has warn, mute, and hackban commands. Additionally, you can create automated actions when users are warned, and can also tempban and softban.

Utility commands

Elevate also has tons of commands to help your server like Reaction Roles, Tickets, and more! Instead of having one bot for reaction roles, one for tickets, one for music, and one fore moderation, you can use Elevate to make your server more professional and consolidate commands.

Customizable

Elevate has customizable behavior. You can change where the bot sends logs, and much more. Plus, you can add and configure prefixes if other bots already use Elevate's prefix, e!.

Welcome Messages

Tired of users joining your server, and not knowing what to do or being intimidated by a large number of channels? Elevate can send a custom welcome message so users never feel lost. Studies have shown that users who are send a welcome message are 1.5x likely to stay in a server than if the didn't receive any message.

Music

Elevate also comes with a fully working audio system with playlists, autoplay, and more!. You can play music from Youtube, Mixer, Soundcloud, Twitch, Bandcamp, and Vimeo, and even more platforms are being added. Not only does music lighten people's mood, it can improve performance while gaming or doing schoolwork.

Logging

Elevate logs every moderation command, so you always know what your mods are doing
